Sacha Jack Stines

Born and raised in Haiti, I have always been surrounded by color, rhythm, and beauty. While my early academic journey led me to study architecture, it was through the lens of a camera that I discovered my true passion. The meticulous attention to detail honed through architectural design seamlessly translated into the art of photography, where composition, structure, and creativity are paramount. With over six years of experience in the photography world, I specializes in capturing life's most treasured moments. Whether it's the joyful celebration of a wedding, the quiet elegance of a maternity shoot, or the energy and personality of portrait sessions, My approach is always deeply personal. Patience, passion, and attention to detail allow me to capture genuine emotions, turning fleeting moments into lasting memories. In addition to my work as a photographer, I am also a dedicated dancer, with a love for Salsa, Bachata, Casino Rueda, and kizomba. I am constantly evolving in my field, seeking new ways to push the boundaries of traditional photography.
